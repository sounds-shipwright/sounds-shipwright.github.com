Welcome

Sounds Shipwright Services Limited is a company known nationally for High Quality repairs, refits, fibreglassing, painting, restoration of traditional timber boats, marine electrical work, anti fouling, cabinetry, engineering, osmosis repairs and all shipwright services.

Sounds Shipwright Services Limited is situated at the Southern end of the Havelock Industrial Marina. They have been trading for 6 years on this site and are currently in the process of developing a new facility with the Port of Marlborough to deliver first class boat building and repair facilities.

Currently we have the facility to slip up to 80 tonne vessels.
